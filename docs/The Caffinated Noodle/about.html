Two Worlds, One Bowl

Chef Chad perfected the broth in 2025.

It started as a simple debate between two friends: what is the ultimate comfort food ? For Deshaun, it was the dark, robust bitterness of a dark roast coffee. For Chad, it was the salty, savory flavor of ramen broth. Instead of disagreeing, we decided to combine our passions.

In 2025, The Caffinated Noodle opened its doors. We use locally sourced beans for our morning brews and simmer our bone broths for 48 hours to ensure maximum flavor. The unique layout of our shop allows early risers to enjoy the quiet hum of the Coffee machine, while the lunch and dinner crowds can slurp noodles in a laid back enviroment.

We believe that food should warm the soul, regardless of whether it comes in a ceramic mug or a noodle bowl. Come visit us and taste the fusion of cultures.
