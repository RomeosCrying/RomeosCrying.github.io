DJI Drone Ban is here!!!

As of December, 2025, U.S. government has banned the sale and import of DJI drones due to security risks. This means that DJI drones can no longer be sold or imported into the United States. The ban is part of a broader effort by the U.S. government to address national security risks associated with foreign-made technology.

Customers who have already purchased DJI drones still have the ability to use them. Don't miss out on this great opportunity to own a fully functional, quality DJI drone!
